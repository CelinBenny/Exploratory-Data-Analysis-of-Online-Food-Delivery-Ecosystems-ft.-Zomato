# Exploratory-Data-Analysis-of-Online-Food-Delivery-Ecosystems-ft.-Zomato

PROJECT OVERVIEW

This project performs an in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a Zomato dataset to understand restaurant performance and customer preference patterns within an online food delivery ecosystem.
The analysis focuses on identifying how factors such as pricing, location, restaurant type, online services, and customer engagement influence ratings and overall success.

OBJECTIVES

* Analyze restaurant performance
* Understand customer preferences
* Study pricing patterns
* Evaluate impact of online services
* Identify potential market opportunities

DATASET
* Source: Zomato Dataset\Kaggle
* Records: ~41,000+ restaurants

FEATURES INCLUDE:
* Restaurant name
* Location
* Ratings and votes
* Cost for two
* Restaurant type and cuisines
* Online ordering and table booking

TOOLS AND TECHNOLOGIES
* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib & Seaborn

KEY INSIGHTS
* The ecosystem is dominated by low and mid-priced restaurants, indicating strong demand for affordability.
* Most restaurants fall within the ₹300–₹700 range, reflecting a mass-market structure.
* Ratings are concentrated between 3.5–4.2, showing a competitive environment.
* Higher-priced restaurants tend to have better ratings, but improvements plateau beyond the premium segment.
* Customers prefer Quick Bites and Delivery, highlighting convenience-driven behavior.
* Experience-driven restaurants (pubs, cafés, microbreweries) achieve the highest ratings.
* Popularity (votes) is linked to consistent quality over time.
* Online ordering has a minor impact on ratings, acting as a supporting factor.
* Location influences performance, with areas like Koramangala and Lavelle Road performing strongly.
* Restaurant success depends on a combination of pricing, experience, location, and consistency.

BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS

* Target the mid-to-high price segment for optimal balance of demand and quality
* Choose high-performing locations for better success probability
* Focus on experience + convenience hybrid concepts
* Avoid overpricing in the luxury segment due to diminishing returns
* Maintain consistent service quality to improve ratings and customer engagement

CONCLUSION

The analysis shows that restaurant success in an online food delivery ecosystem is driven by a balance of affordability, experience, and consistency. While customers frequently choose convenient options, they rate experience-driven restaurants higher, highlighting a gap between usage and satisfaction.
